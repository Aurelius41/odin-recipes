Brownies

Brownies are a supreme delicacy, enjoyed by many. It is a simple dish to whip up.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Cocoa Powder
  • FLour
  • Water
  • Sugar
  • Chocolate

Steps

  1. Mix the wet ingredients together
  2. Combine the dry ingredients in a seperate bowl
  3. Slowly, add the dry mixture to the wet mixture, stirring constantly
  4. Once you've reached the preferred consistency of the mix, add it to a baking tray and set it in the oven for 2 minutes
  5. Once baked, set it out on a plate and enjoy
